# LANDSCAPE-HEALTH POWER BI


Landscape Health
Project Overview:
Developed a comprehensive Power BI dual-dashboard system to monitor and analyze landscape health metrics across multiple zones, tracking shrub and tree conditions for environmental restoration projects.
Dashboard 1 - Shrub Data:
Created KPI cards showing total shrub area (13.43K m²), stressed areas (2.2K), replaced areas (1.052K), recovered areas (1.208K), and gravel additions (3.17K). Built zone-wise comparison charts displaying shrub distribution across Zone 3 (6345 m²), Zone 2 (4144 m²), and Zone 1 (2939 m²). Designed donut charts showing stress vs. recovery metrics with percentage breakdowns and zone-specific stress distribution (Zone 3: 57%, Zone 2: 35%, Zone 1: 8%). Developed bar charts tracking gravel additions and totals by zone.
Dashboard 2 - Tree Category:
Designed KPIs for total area (1.262K), recovery (12.73), replacement (8.20), and stress (16.35). Created comparative visualizations showing Date Palms (936 trees) vs. Trees (326) across health indicators. Built pie charts for category distribution and bar charts tracking recovery, replacement, and stress percentages.
Features:
Interactive zone and category filters, color-coded visualizations, multi-chart coordination, real-time KPI tracking.
Impact:
Enables quick identification of stressed zones, tracks recovery progress, and supports data-driven environmental restoration decisions.
